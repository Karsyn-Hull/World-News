Ukraine claims to have destroyed large Russian ammunition depot in overnight drone attack

By Niamh Kennedy, Maria Kostenko and Victoria Butenko, CNN

Updated 11:57 AM EDT, Wed September 18, 2024

CNN— A Ukrainian drone attack launched overnight destroyed an ammunition depot in the western Russian region of Tver, a source from Ukraine’s Security Service (SBU) told CNN on Wednesday, causing a massive blast. The Ukrainian drones targeted the Russian defense ministry-operated warehouse in the city of Toropets, which was storing Iskander tactical missile systems, Tochka-U tactical missile systems, guided aerial bombs and artillery ammunition, the source said.
